assortment

COHIBA

Behike Fifty-Two

The BEHIKE is the most high-end series of Cohiba brand. For the first time, "Medio Tiempo" tobacco leaves are added to these three series. Medio Tiempo is a very rare tobacco variety. It refers to the upper two layers of tobacco leaves of tobacco plants cultivated by the sun planting method. Due to the unique position of this tobacco leaf on the tobacco plant), it has excellent characteristics and flavor, good combustion and strong concentration make you feel its unique and exquisite flavor.

COHIBA

Behike Short

This is a new addition to the Cuban cigarillo range. Each cigar is individually wrapped in cellophane to ensure the cigars are fully protected and provide optimal freshness when ready to sample. The extra ring gauge provides better opportunities to enjoy unique flavors.

COHIBA

Fifty-Five Aniversario
Limited Edition

Awarded the title of Edición Limitada, Habanos S.A. is an unconventional product with a limited production of 50,000 pieces, each of which is packed into an exclusive humidor (limited number 1-10,000), and 100% selected cigar leaves from the Vuelta Abajo region of Cuba , aged for at least two years and using rare Cohiba filler cigar leaves (Seco and Ligero), followed by additional fermentation to give Cohiba cigars their unique aroma and taste. Finished with delicate footmarks, each has a unique sub-brand with the word 'Premier' printed on it.

Siglo II

The Century series is said to be milder than other Cuban brands of the same size, but they all taste very similar, with mild creamy sweetness, medium-bodied bean flavor and medium-bodied tobacco flavor. The length of 129mm makes this cigar pleasant to the palate.
